Product Architecture
Three layers. One health story.
A patient interface, a local-first intelligence core, and a clinical provider portal — connected through a model-agnostic AI reasoning layer.
Patient Interface

Mobile

iOS • HealthKit • Wearables

Logging, tracking, and management for medications, supplements, fitness, nutrition, and sleep. HealthKit/Google sync, document upload, wearable integrations.

Intelligence Engine

Core

Local-First • Free or SaaS

Longitudinal health repository with EHR integrations, document ingestion, structured health graph, and model-agnostic AI reasoning via MCP.

Clinical Interface

Provider

SOAP Notes • Consult AI

Pre-visit AI interval summary, structured documentation with supporting records, and an AI consult assistant for providers.
