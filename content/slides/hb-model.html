Business Model
Four phases to scale.
Near-term revenue from consumer subscription + provider SaaS. Long-term expansion into RPM billing, marketplace, and remote patient care.
01

Consumer Subscription & Local Installs

$15/month AI health assistant with annual discount. Free tier for individual providers. Includes MCP integration for local AI reasoning.

02

RPM & Institutional Providers

Remote patient monitoring at $60/month billed to insurance. Hospital and clinic integration contracts for institutional deployments.

03

Marketplace & Research

Provider discovery platform, clinical trial recruitment, and de-identified data partnerships with explicit patient consent.

04

Remote Patient Care

Automated assistive services, provider tele-presence for remote evaluations, and family tele-presence for isolated patients.
